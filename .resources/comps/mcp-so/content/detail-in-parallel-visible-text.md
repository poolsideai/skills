Overview
What is In Parallel?

Organizations lose an enormous amount of time to coordination.

Status meetings. Chasing updates. Searching for information. Re-explaining decisions. Keeping everyone aligned as priorities change.

In Parallel reduces that coordination tax by continuously maintaining an up-to-date understanding of goals, projects, decisions, ownership, risks, and progress across the organization.

Through MCP, AI assistants like Claude, ChatGPT, and Copilot can access that shared understanding directly, helping teams get answers grounded in what is actually happening across the business.

How to use In Parallel?

Connect In Parallel to your existing meetings and workflows.

As decisions are made and work progresses, In Parallel automatically maintains a shared operational picture of the organization. Teams can use it directly for visibility and execution, or connect AI assistants through MCP to ask questions about projects, priorities, ownership, risks, blockers, and strategic initiatives.

FAQ

What problem does In Parallel solve?

Most organizations spend significant time keeping people aligned. Information is spread across meetings, chats, documents, emails, and tools, making it difficult to understand what is happening and what has changed. In Parallel continuously maintains that understanding so teams spend less time coordinating and more time executing. ** Why connect AI assistants to In Parallel?**

AI is only as useful as the context it can access. In Parallel gives AI assistants access to the same up-to-date organizational understanding that teams rely on to make decisions. ** Is In Parallel a project management tool?**

No. In Parallel focuses on maintaining a shared understanding of execution across the organization. It works alongside existing tools rather than replacing them.

Can I use In Parallel without AI?

Yes. Many organizations use In Parallel to improve visibility, alignment, and execution even without connecting AI assistants.
